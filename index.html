How We Judge a Habit
What Makes a Habit
Truly Good?
Four layers — each one builds on the last.
1
🎯 What Is a Good Habit?
A habit is considered good only when it is performed with a Purpose (goal motive), at the Right Time, and in a Focused manner.
All three must be present
🎯
Purpose
⏰
Right Time
🧠
Focused
2
😊 Why Emotions Matter
A genuinely good habit produces happiness, not fear or sadness. Your emotional outcome is a quality check on your evaluation.
⚠️ If there is a mismatch
Feeling fear, sadness, or both after a purpose-driven habit — or happiness after a pressure- or pleasure-driven habit — means either the habit was incorrectly logged or you are on the wrong track.
3
💎 Consequence Prediction
The expected consequence should match the habit's quality. Before marking a habit as beneficial, ask yourself honestly:
◆ Was it done with a genuine purpose?
◆ Was it done at the right time?
◆ Was it performed with focus and discipline?
You said Yes, but it wasn't.
Overestimating
You said No, but it was.
Undervaluing
4
⚖️ Making the Right Decision
Your decisions must be consistent with the quality of your habit.
① Done with a Purpose
② Done at the Right Time
③ Performed with Focus
④ Feeling of Happiness
All 4 = YES
✅ Continue
Any = NO
⚠️ Improve / Stop

The Truly Good Habit Formula
When all conditions are present, you have a truly good habit.
✅ Motive Purpose (Goal)
✅ Timing Right Time
✅ Manner Focused
😊 Feeling Happiness (no Fear / Sadness)
💎 Consequence Beneficial
⚖️ Decision ✅ Continue
🗺️ How to Use This App
1
📝 Plan Tab — Set Your Daily Habits
Start here. Add the habits you want to build into your day. Set the name, ideal time, and intented day in a week. This becomes your daily blueprint.
2
📋 Track Tab — Log Your Planned Habits
Each day, tap a habit from your plan to log it. Record your motive (Purpose / Pressure / Pleasure), whether the timing was right, your focus level, what felt after, predict consequence and make a decision
3
➕ Other Habit Option — Log Unplanned Habits
Did something outside your plan? Use the Other Habit option in the Track tab to log any unplanned activity with the same detail — motive, timing, focus, and so on.
4
📊 Progress Tab — See Your Patterns
View your habit streaks, motive trends, timing accuracy, focus levels, good habit rates and the rate of your proper use of time. Understand how your habits are shaping up day by day.
5
🔬 Analysis Tab — Detect Inconsistencies
Deep analysis of your logged data. Evaluate habits against an ideal profile. Identify mismatches between motive and emotion, consequence predictions, and decisions. Check for consistency and procrastination. Analyze time invested versus returns gained.
6
👤 Profile Tab — Your Settings
Manage your account, add and manage profiles, delete profile data, or remove profiles completely.
